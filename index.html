Built on Hard Work

Junk And Cleaning Solutions LLC was founded with a simple mission: make it easy for homeowners and businesses to get rid of unwanted junk without the hassle, stress, or hidden fees that come with other services.

We're a locally owned company that genuinely cares about our community. When we haul away your junk, we do our best to recycle, repurpose, or donate items before sending anything to a landfill.

Whether you have a single couch that needs to go or an entire estate to clear out, we bring the truck, the crew, and the work ethic to get it done right.

  • Upfront pricing — you know the cost before we start
  • On-time arrivals and professional crew
  • We handle all the heavy lifting — you don't lift a finger
  • Responsible disposal: we recycle and donate when possible
  • Residential and commercial jobs of all sizes
  • Licensed LLC with full insurance coverage
