Owner & Founder
Meet the Owner

Meet Alex

Alex started Junk And Cleaning Solutions LLC at just 19 years old — not because it was the easy path, but because he saw a real problem in his community and decided to do something about it.

Growing up, Alex watched family members and neighbors struggle to deal with unwanted junk. Moving companies wouldn't touch it. Dumpster rentals were expensive and complicated. And hauling it yourself meant a truck, a free weekend, and a body that could handle the heavy lifting. Most people just let the clutter sit.

So Alex grabbed a truck, called his mom, and showed up. What started as helping people in his neighborhood quickly grew into a full business — built on showing up on time, being honest about pricing, and not stopping until the job was done right.

At an age when most people are still figuring things out, Alex is running a licensed LLC, earning 5-star reviews, and proving every day that hard work and integrity are enough to build something real.
